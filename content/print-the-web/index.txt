text: [Printed Web](https://printed.web.at.kommons.org) is an ongoing online and physical archive of unique websites from around the world.

While this is a continually growing archive, the goal of Printed Web is to have quarterly zines dedicated to the showcasing of websites and online spaces in a physical, printed format for public distribution.
