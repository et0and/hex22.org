text: [The Museum Without Walls] was a project for the first ever [Merveilles Hyperjam](https://tom.so/hyperjam), created over a three day period within an emulated Macintosh Plus environment. While the sequencing is fairly basic, it was great fun to make work using Hypercard and watch what other people created.
