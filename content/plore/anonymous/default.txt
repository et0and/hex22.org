title: Anonymous

----

text: **Anonymous** was a polymath who wrote hundreds of books about the last known years of life on Earth. His philosophy helped humans survive for a few more years and to accept the end. Although he has never named himself as a [Romantic](romantics), late Romantics believe that he was one of the most significant members of the group.

In his last days he asked his followers to title his last book "Concrete structures about the unexistence of life".
